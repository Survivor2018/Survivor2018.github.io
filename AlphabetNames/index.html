About us

Alphabet Names a great way to personalise your space, with colourful, cheerful, and creative alphabets, made in durable and child-friendly plastic.


Be it your cupboard, room door, decorative wall, drawing board, study desk, or play space; all of these can now become fun with your name or choice of words.


Each alphabet comes with a long lasting double sided foam tape which can be used on any surface, wood, metal, laminates, glass and home walls.


It can be removed without leaving marks on the surface.


Enjoy these attractive alphabets for your kids and their friends. They can also be fun giveaways at birthdays and parties.
